About NRCC Berlin

Awanish
🏅 President & Captain

Leading the Nepali Rhinos Cricket Club Berlin since 2024 with dedication and passion to unite the Nepali cricket community in Germany's capital. His vision: build a club that competes fiercely, welcomes everyone, and flies the Nepali flag with pride.

🇳🇵 Nepal
🇩🇪 Berlin

The Nepali Rhinos Cricket Club Berlin (NRCC Berlin) was founded in 2020 to bring together the Nepali diaspora and cricket lovers in Berlin under one banner.

Our crest features the Great One-Horned Rhino alongside the Berlin Bear — a powerful symbol of two identities united by cricket. The bold red circle represents passion; the cyan text the spirit of our community.

We welcome players of all backgrounds. Whether you're a seasoned cricketer or picking up a bat for the first time, there's always a place for you in the Rhinos family.
